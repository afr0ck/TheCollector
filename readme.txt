				------ Instructions -------
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

+ Movement +
Left:		A
Right:		D
Jump:		Space
Double Jump:	Space (after first jump)
Attack:		Enter (small cooldown active)
Crouch:		S

----------------------------

+ Goal +
Traverse the platformed levels finding coins.
You can't proceed until all 10 coins in the level are found and collected.
Fight off monsters while trying to stay alive.
Reach the final level to find the Ruby to win the game!

----------------------------

+ Player +
Lives:		4
Health:		5 Hearts
Damage:
	Attack:	20 DMG
	Stomp:	10 DMG	

----------------------------

+ Monsters +
